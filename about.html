ABOUT US


Established in 2023 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, Hike Horizon's mission is to help empower fellow explorers like you with the tools and resources needed to take on the great outdoors and discover new adventures. With years of experience we know well the joys and perils of the wilderness, and wish to share our knowledge with others who desire to get out there.


We have curated a range of comprehensive gear checklists tailored to your specific outdoor pursuits. Whether you're a beginner, a seasoned backpacking through hiker or weekend car camper, our checklists will help to ensure you have all the essential gear and equipment needed for a safe and enjoyable experience.


In addition to our gear checklists, we offer a powerful search feature that allows you to easily find hiking trails and campsites near any location. With just a few clicks, you can discover hidden gems in your area or explore new destinations far and wide.


Find the path that calls to you, lace up your boots, strap on your pack and join us! Adventure awaits. Get ready to navigate uncharted territories, check off breathtaking vistas from your bucket list and embark on unforgettable journeys. Let us be your compass in the world of outdoor exploration and guide you in pursuit of new horizons!
